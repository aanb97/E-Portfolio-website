Literature Review on AI in Medical Diagnosis

Summary

In this unit, I learned how to formulate research questions and carry out a literature review (LR). The LR was conducted on the application of artificial intelligence in medical diagnosis.

Reflection

The process of conducting LR proved meaningful and enlightened me, which required a close assessment of the credible sources I used and the key insights and findings I formulated. The research theme centered on AI in medical diagnosis helped to underscore how and to what extent AI has been revolutionizing the healthcare field, with a stress on the necessity for effective ethical frameworks.
