Reflective Activity

Summary

Through this unit, I was able to explore the scientific methods used in academic research and the ethical considerations to follow meticulously as an academic and professional researcher. I was able to differentiate the difference between inductive and deductive reasoning, besides knowing the significance of ethical issues in my domain of research.

Reflection

Understanding the key role of the scientific method in having a proper structure for the research was enormously important. Research ethics, especially in AI research, are significant for making sure AI is used responsibly and no harm is committed.
