Ethical Analysis of Surveys

Summary

Through this unit, I learned how ethical issues can be encountered in the process of data collection and how significant the ethical dimensions are in conducting research. The case study of The Cambridge Analytica scandal was useful throughout, whereas I assessed the ethical implications of such practices.

Findings

The key findings were the misuse of personal data and how it leads to massive breaches of privacy. It was also found that ethical, social, and legal frameworks need to be taken into account while collecting data to safeguard the rights of the users.

Reflection

Ethical considerations have been important to maintain the trust of the public and ensure that legal standards are complied with.
