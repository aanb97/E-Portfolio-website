Dissertation Plan on AI Technologies for Physically Disabled and Elderly

Summary

This unit reflected on how to make research reports and carry out research writing. After learning in this unit, I prepared a comprehensive plan for drafting my research dissertation. My topic for my research dissertation was “AI technologies for assisting the physically disabled and elderly.”

Reflection

This unit helped structure my dissertation plan. It also provided clarity and direction for my research. This unit also stressed the significance of systematic reporting to convey research outcomes effectively.
